# Hi, I'm Samarjit 👋

I'm a Product Manager who builds the tools I wish existed instead of just asking engineering for them.

Most of my day job is product strategy, partner integrations, and AI/ML features on a B2B platform but the repos here are different. They're internal tools and small builds I scripted myself, end to end, usually because a manual process was eating hours every week and nobody had time to fix it properly.

## What's here

- **Workflow automation** — Slack → docs pipelines, release tracking, anything that turns "someone manually compiles this every week" into a script
- **AI-assisted builds** — small tools built with Claude/Cursor as a coding partner, documented honestly about what I wrote vs. what I prompted for
- **Small games** — proof-of-craft builds, not platform work

## How I work

I'm not an engineer by background (7+ years in product, most of it hands-on with SQL/Python for analytics and a fair amount of scripting). The repos here reflect that: working code, clear READMEs, and honest notes on what's automated vs. still manual. I'd rather ship something small and real than something polished and theoretical.
